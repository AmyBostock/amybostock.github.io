Plane Code Fixing Excercise



About

This part of the tutorial was giving you a piece of broken code which caused the plane to erratically fly away from the player and the camera was not attached to the plane asset. The challenge was to take what was learned during the Van Mini Game and fix the broken code.
There was also a bonus challenge to make the propellar on the plane spin whilst moving.

I thoroughly enjoyed learning the techniques here as it really made me have to think about how I was going to fix this code and make the end result a fun little game.
