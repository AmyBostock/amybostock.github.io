Practice Van Mini Game




About

This tutorial covers the basics of coding with C#. You will be required to add code to a test scene and allow the player to control a vehicle and move it down a road, ensuring the camera is also following.
After learning the techniques presented in the Unity tutorial, I began adding more challenge to the simple level. This included more vehicles driving towards the player as well as obstacles to avoid. I also took some techniques from the Plane Code Fix and apply them to this project, including making the vehicle's wheels rotate when the vehicle is moving.




After completing this first prototype, I decided to challenge myself further by adding local split screen multiplayer to the mini game, as well as the ability to swap camera view with a button press.
